Section 508 Procurement Playbook

THIS IS A DRAFT

The implementation of Section 508 standards can be improved by ensuring that contract awards contain strict requirements and incentive payments for achieving Section 508 compliance. The plays listed here are intended help program managers and contracting officers ensure that the government is purchasing Section 508-compliant products and services.

Software Development Contract Plays

  1. Integrate accessibility as an RFP evaluation factor

  2. In the evaluation process, review for contractor's knowledge of Section 508 and past performance

  3. In the award process, tie payment to accessibility attestation by a third party

COTS Plays

  1. In the RFP process state specific standards for accessibility

  2. Tie release of payment to attestation by a third party tester

Hardware Purchasing Plays

  1. Establish standard language for hardware categories

  2. Explicitly state specific standards for accessibility

  3. Ensure that hardware being purchased has the capacity to accommodate accessibility software
